Is this website only for students or anyone?

2006/07/01 19:32:35 PDT by lloyd
Edited at 2006/07/01 19:33:31 PDT

It's mostly for TIC students to use and participate in, yes. I say 'mostly' because anyone else from ATDP (or the internet at large) can read and benefit from the information/discussions.

But yes, use of the site--particularly its forums and chatrooms--is primarily for TIC students. (Note that I'm not saying 'only current students' -- former TIC/AIC/TVA students, TAs and instructors may certainly participate here as much as they want; note the very helpful participation of one 'Forward Biased' ::chuckle::.)

If you have friends or schoolmates in other ATDP classes who have IoCM sites, but which aren't as active as yours -- and have been making envious glances here -- do encourage them to become more pro-active on their own course sites. All it takes is a bit of nudging of their own classmates to get their sites as dynamic as this one.
