Edited at 2006/07/06 13:43:12 PDT

MySpace

  • Anecdotes about the popularity of MySpace (Pono and his cousins, little kids swarming Lloyd's PowerBook at recess, Lloyd is not a kiosk, etc.)

  • People use MySpace because their friends use it (circular reasoning)

  • People use MySpace to keep in touch

  • Wikipedia article on MySpace is "disputed" because so many users have different ideas of what MySpace is

Social Networking

  • Social networking has existed since the caveman days, but the online element is a recent addition

  • Social networks that preceded MySpace: Yahoo! Groups, LiveJournal, etc.

  • Some networks are more popular in certain regions/demographics (Friendster in the Phillipines, Orkut in Brazil, Facebook with college students, etc.)

  • Googlefighting: Only Flickr rivals MySpace in popularity

Future of MySpace

  • 20th Century Fox, The Simpsons, American Idol: all properties of Rupert Murdoch's News Corp.

  • MySpace is now also owned by News Corp. (expect tie-ins with other News Corp. properties)

  • Speculation: social networking services will converge in the future

MySpace and You

  • Someone in your generation will bring about the next big thing in social networking

  • If that person is you, be like Bill Gates, and use your money to help people (eg. Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation)

  • Also, go watch An Inconveninent Truth (The future is yours!)

I'm probably leaving out some of it, but this is all I can remember. I hope this shows that I was paying attention :P
