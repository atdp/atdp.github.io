Tech Support - All technical topics related to TIC go here. Got any questions about the homework or can't figure out how to get your webpage looking just right? Post here and a TA, instructor, or even a fellow student will try to help you out. This is also the place to get friendly constructive feedback on your website work.

Class Discussion - All journal questions will be crossposted here. In addition to writing responses in your own spaces, you should post a reply to the same thread here to encourage discussion amongst the class. Also, if you have another internet related discussion topic that interests you, feel free to start your own thread here to get input from your fellow students.

Off Topic - The section for all topics that aren't related to TIC/ATDP at all. If you have cool websites or links that you want to share, this would be the place for them.
