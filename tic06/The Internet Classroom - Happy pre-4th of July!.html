Photoshop Lab

Make a webpage and place the following six images on it. The two people in class who have the overall funniest/most creative images get to come with us for free pizza today at lunch time. If you're already coming with us because your group won the NetHunt, I'll buy you ice cream after as well. lol. No inappropriate images! If you have to ask, it's probably not appropriate for class.

1) Create an image of President Bush superimposed on a tropical beach, and be sure to add other random characters. File must be saved as a .jpg and be under 275k in size.

2) Superimpose a picture of Kass somewhere in Europe. Save as a .jpg and file must be under 300k in size and over 600-pixels wide.

3) Place Trevor somewhere in an African savannah or city backdrop. Same file constraints as Kass'.

4) Place Lloyd anywhere you like.

5) Create a 32 by 32-pixel icon with a custom made logo. Save as a .gif file.

6) Create a transparent gif of any sort.

Bonus: Find a picture of your TA and create a goofy image with them.
