2006/06/16 10:26:49 PDT by Max Santana

Obsessed is a pretty powerful word to describe it.... I'm not obsessed, only extremely entertained by it. Obsessed would be more like... just barely addicted...

I find that anime is much better than whatever the heck they're showing on TV nowadays, with a few exceptions here and there. Generally speaking, the TV shows lack depth and are frustratingly similar in almost all aspects. Probably also because those shows can be censored and can't really delve into issues even if they wanted to.

And thus anime, or manga, becomes an excellent way to pass the time you would normally have spent watching TV. In comparison, I feel that anime does go beyond the petty and shallow to truly create depth and attachment to the characters. Good drama can rip hearts apart, as some of you probably experienced. And several series actually do touch on modern issues and controversies.

So yeah, I watch anime for entertainment. It's a lot better than what else is out there right now. Not to say I don't watch TV every now and then, CSI and House are pretty good too. But overall, TV shows aren't worth it.
