How to log into the FTP:

Host/IP: 128.32.250.91

Username: First name
Password: First initial + last initial + birthdate (mmddyy) + last letter of your first name + last letter of your last name

ex, if your name is Taesvor Sencoe, and your birthday is 05/16/85, your information will be:
Username: taesvor
Password: ts051685re

...To FTP from home:

First, you may either download an FTP client or use Internet Explorer's built-in FTP abilities, which was what Kass showed at the end. To use IE, first switch the http:// to ftp:// and then your username@server_address. For example, ftp://trevor@128.32.250.91

If you wish to use a seperate FTP client:

Mac: Fetch, Interarchy, Transmit, Cyberduck
PC: SmartFTP, AceFTP, WS_FTP, anything else on download.com for FTP
