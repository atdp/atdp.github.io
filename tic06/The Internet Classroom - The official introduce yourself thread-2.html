But even better than food and music is sweet fellowship with my amigos. I rarely skip out on a chance to hang out with the people whose presence I enjoy.

Other than that, my other interests lie in art (making and appreciating, though I haven't done much of the former as of late), television (haven't watched in awhile), games (haven't played in awhile), volleyball (haven't played in awhile, but read much about it), the piano (haven't played this awhile either), among other things. You might have noticed all the parentheses where I state I haven't done something in awhile, which is true. I'll stick with the age old excuse, "I don't have time." And I don't (but I'm still doing things I love and other things I don't >_>).

So I leave my introduction with this: Do not waste your time, because when all is said and done, it truly is precious. These next six weeks go by fast and so I encourage you all to be pro-active, talk to some people, make a few friends, learn and grow and just have a bit of fun. I thoroughly enjoyed my time with TIC/AIC/TVA, but it's really up to you to make the most of it and this goes for anything, whether it's school or work, etc. Well, I do hope all of you will enjoy your brief time here as I have done in previous years. I don't mind chatting, so feel free to bother me. You can ask me anything (but you might not always get an answer), like asking me to try and convince your instructors to give you a better grade. I can't guarantee they will listen, but hey, who knows? ;D

Now I've overstayed my welcome I believe, so I'll just slip out and interrupt at another time. See you online.
