Journal #2

For this journal entry, think about how you can tell whether information you find on the Internet is fact or fiction. Watch the following video before answering the questions below: The Making of "Where the Hell is Matt?" (Allow for about 15 minutes viewing time.)

Had you seen Matt's dancing video before? If so, how did you hear about it? Did you think it was a hoax when you first finished watching it (either earlier, or as presented in the linked video)? What do you think now that you've seen Matt's "making of" explanation? Consider that Internet memes and viral videos like Matt's can spread very quickly: in a broader sense, what do you think this says about a viewer's exposure to hoaxes, rumors, conspiracy theories, and so on? What do you think should be done (if anything) about the spread of false information on the Internet, and why?
