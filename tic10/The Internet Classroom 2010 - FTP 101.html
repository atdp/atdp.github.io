Using an FTP Program

On the TMF lab computers: Start Menu > All Programs > Networking > WinSCP SFTP.

Host name: atdpmanila.berkeley.edu
Username: First initial + last name
Password: First initial + last initial + birthdate (mmddyy)

Ex, if your name is James Bond, and your birthday is 05/16/85, your login information will be:
Username: jbond
Password: jb051685

Under "Protocol", make sure you change it to "FTP". And make sure you're putting your files in the "Sites" folder!

To FTP from home:

Download an FTP client at home to use. There are many free ones:

Mac: Fetch, Interarchy, Transmit, Cyberduck
Windows: WinSCP FTP (the program we use in the lab), AbsoluteFTP, SmartFTP, AceFTP, WS_FTP, anything else on download.com for FTP

Another way to FTP (requires no downloading!)

There's a neat website called Net2FTP that will let you FTP through your web browser. It can be a little clunky for uploading, but not having to download a program is pretty cool.
