Journal #1

What is your handle (or screen name, or alias) for most sites/IMing/E-mail? Does this internet persona differ much from you in real life? In other words, are you overall the same person online as you are in real life? I know lots of people who are a lot more outgoing and crazy online than they are in real life, and sometimes the case can be the opposite. If this is the case for you, why do you think these differences occur for you? Are you more creative in one world than the other? What is it about the Internet or real life that changes the way people express themselves or their ideas?
