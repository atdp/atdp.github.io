Journal #4

Today's journal is going to be a little existential. :x

On Monday, we learned that the Internet was born from a Cold War military program. If there had been no Cold War, how else do you think the Internet might have been invented? Do you think military initiative would be required for developing technology like TCP/IP, or would the Internet eventually have risen from a university or private company? What if the Internet had never developed at all? Take these scenarios and describe how you think your life would be different. Are there aspects of your life that would be worse without the Internet as we know it? Would any be better?
